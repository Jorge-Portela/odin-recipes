Scrambled Eggs and Cheese

Scrambled Eggs with Cheese is a simple, quick, and delicious dish, perfect for any meal. Made with beaten eggs, melted cheese, and a pinch of salt, it turns out creamy and flavorful. Ideal for those looking for convenience without compromising on taste!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tablespoon grated cheese (or a piece of chopped cheese)
  • Salt to taste*
  • 1 teaspoon butter or oil

Steps

  1. Heat the pan: Place the butter or oil in a frying pan and heat over medium heat.
  2. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a fork until well mixed. Season with a little salt.
  3. Cook the eggs: Pour the beaten eggs into the pan. Stir constantly with a spatula.
  4. Add the cheese: When the eggs are almost done (still a little runny), add the cheese and keep stirring until it melts.
  5. Serve: Once the eggs are creamy, transfer to a plate and enjoy!

* Excessive salt consumption is associated with various health problems, such as high blood pressure, cardiovascular diseases, and kidney issues. The World Health Organization (WHO) recommends a maximum intake of 5 grams of salt per day (equivalent to about 2 grams of sodium).
