Peanut Butter Cookies

There's nothing like a peanut butter cookie to satisfy your sweet tooth and lift your spirits. This peanut butter cookie recipe will quickly become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (250g) peanut butter (smooth or crunchy)
  • 1 cup (200g) sugar (granulated or brown sugar works)
  • 1 large egg

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix the ingredients: In a bowl, combine the peanut butter, sugar, and egg. Mix until smooth and well combined.
  3. Shape the cookies: Scoop out tablespoon-sized portions of dough, roll them into balls, and place them on the baking sheet. Use a fork to press down on each ball, creating a crisscross pattern.
  4. Bake: Bake for 10-12 minutes, or until the edges are lightly golden. Let them cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
  5. Enjoy: That's it! You have delicious, chewy peanut butter cookies in no time.
